Project Idea

Overview

My idea for the project is an application to help you organise meals for the week that will ultimately serve to help to reduce food waste. The way this will work is you enter the ingredients you plan on purchasing for a meal or two along with ingredients you already have on hand, the app will then offer suggestions of meals that use most of the same ingredients while taking into account things like how much of each ingredient is in the pack when purchased from the store.

Motivation

The motivation behind this idea comes in two parts. Firstly, one of the most annoying tasks in my weekly schedule is planning my meals for the week. It’s something I have to sit down and really think about. Having an app that can help me choose other meals to cook throughout the week will be a time saver and give me one less thing to worry about.
Secondly, and more importantly, food waste is a massive problem in society. Globally one third of all food is wasted and in Australia alone around 7.3 million tonnes of food goes to waste. This app would help to alleviate waste within everyone’s home and ultimately within our society.

Description

As mentioned in my overview, at a base level the app will function by looking at what ingredients you plan on purchasing as well as any you have in your home already that you would like to use, analysing how much of each ingredient will be used based off what you plan on making and then suggest other meals that use some or all of the same ingredients. As an example, if you plan on making burgers using mince, tomato, lettuce, cheese and the buns, the app will look at the ingredients listed and suggest that tacos as they would also use mince, tomato, mince, lettuce, and cheese. The recipe list will be achieved two ways, firstly there will be a database with recipes that the app will have access to, these will be common dishes found around the world, you will be able to add common dishes that you like to a shortlist that the app will prioritise suggesting. The second way is for the user to add their own recipes that the app will then add to the priority list for suggestions.

The app will also have filters for personal taste, allergies, and dietary preferences such as vegetarian, vegan, lactose intolerant, nut allergy, etc, so that suggestions that don’t suit your lifestyle won’t appear. The app will generate a shopping list for you as well meaning that you won’t have to write the list yourself.

When it comes to custom recipes, you will have the ability to share any recipes you create with others. You can either make them public, so anyone looking for meal ideas can find it, or your can make them private so only yourself and anyone you share with will have the recipe show up as a suggestion.

Integration with shopping apps (Coles, Woolworths, and any others available) will also be introduced so that once you have your meals planned, you can send everything you need to purchase to the shopping app to allow you to place a click and collect order if that is your preferred method of shopping, rather than having to manually carry over everything you need to buy. Included with this integration will be suggestions of recipes based off of weekly specials and in season recipes that would normally be found on the supermarket’s website or in a magazine.

Skills required

There are going to be a wide range of skills that are required for this. The ability to code in Java for making the app will be essential. Along with coding, the ability to set up a server for the app to talk to (both setting up the physical side of the server as well as all the software too) will be a high priority. Another skill that will be required is graphic design so the app will look polished on the user’s side. These skills coming together will help the app to achieve everything it can be.

Outcome

The outcome of this app being successful will be that overall food waste is reduced in each household that uses this app. The more people that use the app, the more food that will not go to waste. I do not think that this app will be the sole reason that food waste starts to decline, however every little bit helps. The other aspect of this app that will mean it’s successful is everyone that uses having an easier time planning their weekly meals, which will ultimately give them more free hours in a week.
